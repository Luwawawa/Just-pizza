JUST (really good) PIZZA

About us

Just Pizza, founded by Steven Che and Jan Natividad, opened in East Vancouver in August 2024. We wanted to create a pizza shop that feels familiar like your favourite chain but with fine dining quality. Steven, with his fine dining experience working at Michelin-starred restaurants and a renowned sourdough bakery in Oslo, is back home in Vancouver to share his passion for food. His dedication to quality shines through in every slice. Jan, a product marketer in tech, is obsessed with customer experience and storytelling. His goal is to wow every customer and to give back to the community he now calls home. We, re driven by a shared purpose: to feed our community delicious food. When Vancouverites think of getting takeout, we want people to think of Just Pizza.

Find us

Just Pizza in East Vancouver serves elevated classic and adventurous toppings on a freshly made sourdough crust. We hope to become your go-to answer to the question, “What do you feel like eating?”
